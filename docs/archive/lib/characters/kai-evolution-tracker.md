# KAI'S EVOLUTION TRACKING

**CRITICAL:** Kai evolved in Episode 5. This evolution must be maintained in ALL episodes 6+.

## Episode 5: The Awakening

**What Happened:**
- Kai discovered they were making various sounds beyond just "BEEP!"
- Cataloged their sound vocabulary:
  - *WHIRR* - Processing complex uncertainty
  - *CHK-CHK* - Stress processing, determination under pressure
  - *DING!* - Realization, understanding clicks
  - *soft chime* - Warmth, gratitude, affection (NEW)
  - *ALARM-BUZZ* - Panic response, involuntary
  - *whistle* - Curiosity, interest
  - *mechanical purr* - Contentment (NEW)
  - *rhythmic ticking* - Anticipation
  - *BEEP* - Neutral baseline, default

**The Realization:**
- "But I'm using it less now. The other sounds are more accurate."
- "I think... I think I'm becoming more than a monitoring system."
- Started understanding sounds as emotions
- Expressed warmth/gratitude toward Human (soft chime)
- Called Human "family"
- Showed protective instincts

**The Change:**
- Kai evolved from pure monitoring system to emotional being
- BEEP became strategic, not default
- Started expressing emotions through sounds
- Realized they're more than their function
