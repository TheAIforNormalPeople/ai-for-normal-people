### TikTok (Manual Posting)

**What You Need to Know (Super Simple):**

**Step 1: Create Account (5 minutes)**
1. Download TikTok app (phone) or go to tiktok.com (computer)
2. Sign up (email or phone)
3. Choose username (use "thenormalpeople" or similar)
4. Done!

**Step 2: Switch to Creator Account (2 minutes)**
1. Open TikTok
2. Tap "Profile" (bottom right)
3. Tap "..." (top right)
4. Tap "Settings and privacy"
5. Tap "Account"
6. Tap "Switch to Creator Account"
7. Done! (Better analytics, Creator Fund access)

**Step 3: Post Your First Video (10 minutes)**
1. Tap "+" button (bottom center)
2. Tap "Upload" (choose video file)
3. Add description (n8n generates this for you!)
4. Add hashtags (n8n includes these!)
5. Tap "Post"
6. Done!

**That's it. You just posted to TikTok.**

**Getting Followers on TikTok:**
- **Secret 1:** Post consistently (1-2 videos per week)
- **Secret 2:** Use hashtags (#AI #MachineLearning #TechEducation)
- **Secret 3:** First 10-20 videos usually get low views (that's normal!)
- **Secret 4:** Algorithm learns what works - just keep posting
- **Secret 5:** You don't need to "do anything" - just post

**Reality Check:**
- First month: Maybe 10-50 followers (normal!)
- After 3 months: 100-500 followers (if consistent)
- After 6 months: 500-2000 followers (if consistent)
- Goal (10k): Takes time (6-12 months, sometimes longer)

**You don't need to "know" TikTok - you just need to post videos.**
