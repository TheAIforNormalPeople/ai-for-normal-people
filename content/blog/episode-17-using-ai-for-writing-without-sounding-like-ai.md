**What AI is GOOD at:**

- Creating outlines and structure

**What AI is BAD at:**

1. AI helps brainstorm and outline
2. YOU write the actual content (in your voice)
3. AI helps edit and refine
4. YOU add personal touches and examples
5. Final polish (you, not AI)

The key: AI handles structure and research, YOU handle voice and creativity!
