## Key Principles

1. **Balance:** Don't make every episode about Vector's trauma. Mix normal teaching with hints.

2. **Subtlety:** Hints should be subtle at first, building gradually. Readers should notice patterns but not be overwhelmed.

3. **Normal Function:** Vector still teaches. He still functions. The hints are background, not foreground (until Episode 50).

4. **Support System:** Team notices and supports but doesn't push. Shows healthy relationships.

5. **Educational Value:** Even trauma-arc episodes should teach something valuable about AI.
