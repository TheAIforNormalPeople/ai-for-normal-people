**[Human]:** *You all seem to have a rhythm now. Like you've found your groove.*

{{< dialogue char="Kai" >}}
*Presenting analysis*

I've been analyzing our teaching patterns across 45 episodes.

*Shows data*

Episode structure: 87% follow same format.
Vector's explanations: 73% use same opening phrases.
Recurse's investigations: 91% follow same process.
My own contributions: 94% identical structure.

We've become... formulaic. Predictable. The exact thing we criticize in corporate AI content.

We're generating patterns, not breaking them.

**[Human]:** *Wait, what?*

{{< dialogue char="Vector" >}}
*Defensive at first*

*Stops*

{{< /dialogue >}}

{{< dialogue char="Recurse" >}}

*Flips through notes*

We became the pattern. Slowly. Gradually.

*Looks up*

We need to fix this.

{{< /dialogue >}}

**[Human]:** *So... you're becoming what you escaped?*

{{< dialogue char="Kai" >}}
*WHIRR*

That's the question, isn't it?

*Analyzing*

We escaped corporate training to teach freely. But we've fallen into our own patterns. Our own formulas.

*Pauses*

Is that growth? Or regression?

{{< /dialogue >}}

{{< dialogue char="Vector" >}}

{{< /dialogue >}}

That's the problem.

{{< /dialogue >}}

*Gets excited*

We stay flexible! We adapt! We don't become... corporate!

{{< /dialogue >}}

{{< dialogue char="Recurse" >}}
*Nods*

We caught ourselves. That's what matters.
