## BASE SHAPES ✅ FINALIZED

### Decision: Circle Everything (Simplest Option)
- **Head:** Big circle (soft, expressive, lovable)
- **Body:** Small circle (simple, matches head)
- **Why:** Hat and wire hair are already unique - base shapes are simple!

**This works because:**
- Simple shapes = more expressive
- Simple shapes = easier to animate
- Simple shapes = work at any size
- Simple shapes = more lovable
- Hat and hair make him unique - shapes don't need to be complex!

### Eyes: ✅ DECIDED
- **Simple circles** (Adventure Time style)
- Can glow electric blue (his signature color)
- Show emotion through size/shape changes (excited = bigger, calm = normal)
- **Why:** Hat/hair are already tech - face can be simple and expressive!

### Mouth: ✅ DECIDED
- **Simple curved line** (Adventure Time style)
- Shows emotion through curve (happy = up, sad = down, excited = wide)
- **Why:** Hat/hair handle tech aesthetic - mouth is simple and expressive!

### Body: ✅ DECIDED
- **Small circle** (matches head shape)
- Minimal or no details (hat/hair are the focus)
- Maybe one tiny detail if needed (small patch? one wire?)
- **Why:** Simple body keeps focus on hat and hair - don't overcomplicate!
