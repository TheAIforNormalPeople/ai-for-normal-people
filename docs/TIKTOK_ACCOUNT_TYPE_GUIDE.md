## ğŸ“± How to Switch to Creator Account

### Step 1: Create TikTok Account (If New)

1. Download TikTok app (phone) or go to tiktok.com (computer)
2. Sign up (email or phone)
3. Choose username
4. Done!

### Step 2: Switch to Creator Account (2 Minutes)

**On Phone (Easiest):**

1. **Open TikTok**
   - Tap "Profile" (bottom right)

2. **Open Settings**
   - Tap "..." (three dots, top right)
   - Tap "Settings and privacy"

3. **Switch Account Type**
   - Tap "Account"
   - Tap "Switch to Creator Account" or "Switch to Professional Account"
   - (If you see "Switch to Professional Account", that's the same thing - choose "Creator")

4. **Choose Category**
   - Select "Education" or "Tech" or "Science"
   - Tap "Next"

5. **Done!**
   - You're now a Creator Account!

**On Computer:**

1. Go to tiktok.com
2. Click "Profile" (top right)
3. Click "Settings" (gear icon)
4. Click "Account"
5. Click "Switch to Creator Account"
6. Choose category: "Education" or "Tech"
7. Done!
