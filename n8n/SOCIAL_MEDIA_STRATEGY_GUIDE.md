## Twitter & Bluesky for AI for Normal People

**Purpose:** How to use social media effectively to grow your blog, not just post links  
**Focus:** Engagement, community building, and driving traffic that converts

---

## üéØ THE BIG PICTURE

**You're Not Just Posting - You're Building:**
- **Community** - People who care about your characters
- **Trust** - Show you're helpful, not just promotional
- **Traffic** - Drive people to your blog (where you make money)
- **Email List** - Your most valuable asset

**Current Problem:**
- Just posting episode links = low engagement
- No personality = people don't connect
- No engagement = algorithm doesn't show your posts
- No community = no one shares or comes back

**Solution:**
- Mix of content types (not just links)
- Character voice in posts
- Engage with replies and comments
- Build relationships, not just broadcast

---

## üì± PLATFORM DIFFERENCES

### Twitter/X
- **Algorithm:** Favors engagement (replies, retweets, likes)
- **Best for:** Threads, quick updates, real-time conversations
- **Character limit:** 280 characters (or threads)
- **Audience:** Larger, more diverse, more competitive
- **Timing:** Multiple times per day works

### Bluesky
- **Algorithm:** Chronological feed (less algorithmic manipulation)
- **Best for:** Longer posts, community building, niche audiences
- **Character limit:** 300 graphemes
- **Audience:** Smaller but more engaged, tech-savvy
- **Timing:** 1-2 times per day is fine

## üé≠ CONTENT STRATEGY

### 1. CONTENT MIX (80/20 Rule)

**80% Value, 20% Promotion**

**Value Content (80%):**
- Character quotes (funny Vector/Kai/Recurse lines)
- Quick AI tips (actionable, not just links)
- Behind-the-scenes (writing process, character development)
- Threads breaking down concepts (from episodes)
- Answering questions (help people)
- Sharing resources (tools, articles, insights)

**Promotion (20%):**
- New episode announcements
- Episode links (with context, not just "new post!")
- Email list signups (soft, not pushy)
- Community invites (when you build one)

**Example Mix (Per Week):**
- 2 episode links (promotion)
- 3-4 character quotes (value + personality)
- 2-3 quick tips (value)
- 1-2 threads (value + promotion)
- 1 behind-the-scenes (value + personality)
- Daily engagement (replies, comments)

## ‚è∞ POSTING SCHEDULE

### Twitter/X
**Frequency:** 3-5 times per day (mix of content types)  
**Best Times:** 
- 8-10 AM (people checking before work)
- 12-1 PM (lunch break)
- 5-7 PM (after work)
- 9-11 PM (evening scroll)

**Strategy:**
- Morning: Quick tip or character quote
- Midday: Thread or episode link
- Evening: Engagement (replies, comments)
- Night: Behind-the-scenes or resource share

### Bluesky
**Frequency:** 1-2 times per day  
**Best Times:**
- 10-11 AM (tech audience is active)
- 2-3 PM (afternoon check-in)

**Strategy:**
- Morning: Episode link or longer post
- Afternoon: Character quote or tip

## üí¨ ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

### Daily Engagement (30-60 minutes)

**What to Do:**
1. **Reply to comments** - Every comment gets a response (as characters if possible)
2. **Engage with others** - Reply to AI-related posts, share insights


## üéØ GROWTH TACTICS

### 1. HASHTAGS (Use Sparingly)
**Twitter:** 1-2 relevant hashtags max
- #AIWriting
- #AITools
- #ChatGPT
- #ClaudeAI
- #AIContent

**Bluesky:** Less important (chronological feed)

### 2. ENGAGE WITH INFLUENCERS
**Strategy:**
- Follow AI educators, writers, tech people
- Reply to their posts (add value, don't just say "great post!")
- Share their content (with your take)
- Build relationships over time

**Don't:**
- Spam them with your links
- Ask for retweets
- Be pushy or salesy

### 3. JOIN CONVERSATIONS
**Strategy:**
- Search for AI-related questions
- Answer helpfully (with links when relevant)
- Share insights, not just links
- Build reputation as helpful expert

### 4. CROSS-PLATFORM PROMOTION
**Strategy:**
- Mention Bluesky on Twitter ("Also on Bluesky: @thenormalpeople")
- Mention Twitter on Bluesky ("Also on Twitter: @thenormalpeople")
- Drive traffic between platforms
