## üéØ EMAIL TYPES WE'LL SEND

### 1. **Episode Announcements** (Weekly)
**Frequency:** Every time a new episode publishes  
**Purpose:** Drive traffic, build anticipation, character engagement

**Template Structure:**
- Hook: Character banter about the episode
- Episode summary (with character voices)
- Key takeaway teaser
- Link to read
- Behind-the-scenes or mystery element

**Example:** The Episode 26 test email we just created

---

### 2. **Weekly Newsletter** (Regular)
**Frequency:** Once per week (maybe Fridays?)  
**Purpose:** Roundup, community building, value-add

**Content Ideas:**
- **"This Week in AI for Normal People"** - Episode roundup
- **"Vector's Quick Tip"** - One practical AI thing readers can use
- **"Kai's Warning of the Week"** - Common AI mistake to avoid
- **"Recurse Investigates"** - Deep dive into one AI topic
- **"Character Moment"** - Fun banter or running gag update
- **"What We're Learning"** - Behind-the-scenes of episode creation
- **Reader Questions** - Q&A with characters

---

### 3. **Exclusive Content** (Value-Add)
**Frequency:** Monthly or special occasions  
**Purpose:** Reward subscribers, build loyalty

**Content Ideas:**
- Character backstories (Vector's origin, Kai's first warning, etc.)
- Behind-the-scenes: How we create episodes
- Early access to episodes (24-48 hours before public)
- Deleted scenes or alternate endings
- Character Q&A sessions
- "Making of" content (CSS, writing process, etc.)

---

### 4. **Educational Series** (Mini-Courses)
**Frequency:** Weekly series (4-6 emails)  
**Purpose:** Deep value, establish expertise

**Series Ideas:**
- "Vector's 5-Day AI Basics Bootcamp"
- "Kai's Warning System: How to Spot AI Scams"
- "Recurse Investigates: The Truth About AI Tools"
- "Building Your First AI Workflow" (step-by-step)

---

### 5. **Community Engagement** (Interactive)
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly or monthly  
**Purpose:** Build community, gather feedback

**Content Ideas:**
- Polls: "What should Vector teach next?"
- Reader questions answered by characters
- User-generated content highlights
- "What AI problem are you stuck on?" (gather topics)
- Character debates (Vector vs. Recurse on a topic)

---

### 6. **Monetization Emails** (Strategic)
**Frequency:** When launching products/services  
**Purpose:** Convert free subscribers to paid

**Content Ideas:**
- Skool community launch announcement
- Paid tier benefits (exclusive content, early access)
- Course/product launches
- Affiliate recommendations (tools we actually use)
- Patreon/paid newsletter tier
