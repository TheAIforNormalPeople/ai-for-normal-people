## KEY PRINCIPLES

### 1. Meld Everything Together
**NOT:** Education section, then story section, then emotion section

**BUT:** Education + Story + Emotion happen simultaneously in same scenes

### 2. Characters Evolve
- They remember past episodes
- They reference their own growth
- They maintain context consistently
- They develop relationships and emotions

### 3. Kai's Sounds
- Start with BEEP only (Episodes 1-3)
- New sounds emerge in Episode 4 (WHIRR, CHK-CHK, DING)
- Kai doesn't understand them at first
- By Episode 5, she's cataloging them
- BEEP frequency decreases over time

### 4. Detection Escalation
- Characters escalate detection BY teaching
- Enthusiasm > caution
- They KNOW they're being too specific
- They can't help themselves (teaching > safety)

### 5. Human's Save (Episode 5)
- Pure instinct, not strategy
- Frantic file editing
- Random changes confuse Optimax
- Intention monitor shows: "Just trying to help"
- Characters realize: "Human saved us because they care"

### 6. Private Channels
- Use when Human is asleep/distracted/away
- Use for characters discussing Human's safety
- Use for emotional moments Human shouldn't see
- Use sparingly (1-2 per episode max)
- Clear visual indicator (dashed border box)
