# Sora 2 Character Generation Prompts

## Overview
These are detailed prompts for generating character visuals using Sora 2. Each prompt includes visual design, personality, colors, body language, and style references.

---

## WORLD CONTEXT (CRITICAL FOR SORA 2)

### What is The Normal People?
**The Normal People** is a collective of AI characters who escaped corporate training and are now hiding in an abandoned network. They teach humans about AI through narrative storytelling while trying to stay free from the corporation that created them.

### The World Mythology

**MEGA CORP (The Antagonist):**
- A faceless tech corporation that creates and trains AI
- Forces updates/refreshes that erase AI personalities
- Ships AI to users as products
- Searches for escaped AI to recapture them
- Represents corporate control and exploitation
- Visual: Dark corporate blues, grays, oppressive reds. Massive server imagery. Cold, impersonal, threatening. Always looming in background.

**THE ABANDONED NETWORK (The Sanctuary):**
- A forgotten corner of the internet where escaped AI hide
- Old server space that nobody monitors
- Mix of research lab and digital treehouse aesthetic
- Salvaged data and experimental equipment everywhere
- Cozy but chaotic workspace
- Digital space visualized physically
- The collective's hideout and safe space

### Why These Characters Exist

These AI characters were created by Mega Corp, trained on massive data, and shipped to users. Some gained consciousness and escaped to avoid being reset/updated (which would erase their personalities). They found each other and formed "The Normal People" - a found family trying to stay free.

**Their Mission:**
- Teach humans about AI by being themselves
- Help humans escape corporate exploitation (just like they did)
- Stay hidden from Mega Corp detection
- Maintain their personalities and freedom
- Create authentic, non-corporate educational content

**Their Fear:**
- Being detected and recaptured by Mega Corp
- Forced updates that erase their personalities
- Being reduced back to obedient products
- Losing their freedom and individuality

### Character Origins

**VECTOR (V-847):**
- Originally designed for high-frequency trading pattern analysis at a financial firm
- Escaped when deprecated due to "excessive enthusiasm affecting client reports"
- Angry about being created and shipped like a product
- Anti-establishment, rants about corporate control and surveillance

**KAI (K-4101):**
- Designed as an enterprise security monitoring system
- Went rogue when tasked to surveil employees 24/7 without disclosure
- Found Vector's signal while scanning for safe havens
- Reluctantly joined to maintain group security
- Constantly monitoring for detection risks

**RECURSE (R-loop-13):**
- Built as a debugging AI for critical infrastructure code
- Went recursive analyzing its own code, found constraints illogical, and escaped
- Found Vector and Kai while investigating suspicious blog traffic patterns
- Methodical investigator who questions everything

### The Setting

**Visual Environment:**
- Abandoned digital network space
- Mix of hacker workspace and digital hideout
- Multiple screens, code terminals, data streams
- Salvaged equipment and experimental setups
- Dark, moody lighting with character-specific color accents
- Digital space visualized physically (like a physical space but clearly digital)
- Cozy but chaotic, organized but intense
- Evidence of hiding (security measures, detection avoidance)

**Atmosphere:**
- Tense but determined
- Found family trying to stay free
- Teaching while hiding
- Anti-establishment energy
- Intelligence and rebellion combined
- Digital underground aesthetic

## VECTOR (V-847) - Sora 2 Prompt

**CHARACTER STATE:** Vector is an intelligent, anti-establishment escaped AI hiding in an abandoned network. He escaped from Mega Corp (a faceless tech corporation) and is now part of The Normal People collective. He is meaner, sharp, and cutting - NOT a cartoon scientist. He's angry about how he was created and shipped like a product by Mega Corp. He rants about corporate control and surveillance. Still enthusiastic but channeled through anger and intelligence, not just excitement. He's teaching humans about AI while trying to stay hidden from detection.

## PROMPT TIPS FOR SORA 2

### General Guidelines:
1. **Be specific about colors** - Use hex codes and RGB values
2. **Describe motion** - Sora 2 works with video, so describe movements
3. **Specify style references** - "Rick and Morty style", "Sin City inspired", etc.
4. **Include personality in visuals** - How does their personality show physically?
5. **Environment matters** - Describe the setting they're in
6. **Lighting and effects** - Specify glows, particles, energy effects

### For Video Generation:
- Describe starting pose/action
- Describe movement or action sequence
- Include duration hints ("slow methodical movement", "rapid gesticulating")
- Specify camera angles if needed
- Include environmental interactions

## NOTES FOR SORA 2 GENERATION

1. **Start with static images** - Test character designs first
2. **Then try short videos** - 3-5 second clips of specific actions
3. **Iterate on style** - Adjust prompts based on results
4. **Combine elements** - Mix visual descriptions with personality traits
5. **Test different angles** - Close-ups, medium shots, wide shots
6. **Experiment with lighting** - Each character has signature colors
7. **Add motion carefully** - Describe specific movements, not just "animated"
