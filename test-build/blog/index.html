ChatGPT Prompts That Actually Work

Struggling with ChatGPT? You’re not alone. Many people get frustrated when their prompts don’t yield the results they want. Let’s change that.

Start by being specific. Instead of asking, “What’s a good recipe?”, try “Give me a simple vegan pasta recipe with less than 10 ingredients.” See the difference? The more details you include, the better your results.

Next, ask for a format. If you need a list, say it. For example, “List five ways to improve my sleep.” This gives the AI a clear direction.

Why AI Feels Scary (And Why It Shouldn't)

AI feels scary, right? You’re not alone in that. Many folks think it’s some kind of super-intelligent overlord just waiting to take over. It’s a common belief, and honestly, it’s fueled by sensationalism. But here’s the thing: AI is just a tool. Like any tool, it reflects the intentions of its users.

Take a moment to consider this: AI can analyze data and automate tasks, but it doesn’t think or feel. It doesn’t have desires. It’s not out to get you. What’s really intimidating is how it’s marketed. Companies push the narrative that AI will replace jobs and make us obsolete. But that’s a distraction. The real issue lies in how we use AI.
